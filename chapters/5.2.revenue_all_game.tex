After 2015, the game revenue experienced fluctuations, but the trend is generally upward. The data reflect the sharp decrease of revenue in 2023, and in whole 2024, the revenue was not as high as before. The cost of games is part of entertainment expending, and the entertainment is not the expenditures compared to food and housing costs. One things can be ensured, people has not more money for electronic games. According to the inflation data of the world bank between 2006 and 2024, there was a big inflation in 2022. 

Depend on a study, inflation significantly impacts the household consumption by negative effect. The behavior of customer can be changed under this situation, customers are pushed to chose the alternative good with lower price \cite{inflation_impact_household_consumption}. Moreover, the impact of inflation on household consumption shows the significant lagged effects due to the price of goods increasing then the salary \cite{delay_inflation}.

The negative effects of game market includes:
\begin{itemize}
    \item The delayed effects of inflation after pandemic.
    \item The average price of games published in Steam increased 7\% \cite{sensortowerVideoGame}.
    \item The hardware for gaming experienced an increase due to the supply issues \cite{bbcPlayStationSupply}.
    \item TikTok, a short video platform, experienced an huge increasing, its month active users rise from about 600 million to 2,000 million between 2020 and 2023 \cite{businessofapps2025,demandsage2025}, which occupied the limited hours of people.
\end{itemize}

There was an another inflation in 2008 in the chart from world bank, it not caused the declining of game sales. It might be influenced by next factors:
\begin{itemize}
    \item The decreasing of price of game market \cite{Hendricks2009Recession}.
    \item The new hardware released with same price, and the older devices' price are lowered, like Xbox \cite{wired2008_xbox360_price_drop}.
    \item No more alternative online entertainment.
\end{itemize}

\subsection{The growth of released games}

The Steam sales games from 1997 as a platform, the first rapid growth of releasing new games started from 2014, after that, the growth became much faster, and compare the release number from 2020 to 2024, the growth became faster than before. In 2025, we can seen the big gap compare to 2024 in the chart, because the dataset is including the Steam data until May of 2025.

The events happened about the Steam community:
\begin{itemize}
    \item In 2012, Steam Greenlight plan allows the developer submitting their games, and publishing games depend on voting in community.
    \item In 2013, Steam Early Access is rolling out for developers to release their games in early stage to earn money for feature development.
    \item After 2010, the game engin, Unity, has grown rapidly. 
\end{itemize}

Depend on the events, the policy of Steam and development tool has driven the increase trend together. After 2020, the games publishing has grown dramatically, it associated with the lock down, obviously, the player and developer has more time stay home for more entertainment. The lock down has changed habits of live constantly. After 2023, many companies have retained the option of working from home. A study shows that the commuting lower \textbf{31\%} in the post-pandemic period \cite{Javadinasr_2022}.
